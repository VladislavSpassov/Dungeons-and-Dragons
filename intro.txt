===============================================
              Dungeons & Pythons!              
===============================================

Welcome to the game!

You, The Hero (H), will be spawned in a dungeon
with the goal to reach the exit gate (G) alive. 
Walking its halls (.) you will stumble 
upon useful treasures (T) that will aid you 
throughout your adventure.
And don't worry you won't be lonely inside.
Behind every thick wall (#) lurks an enemy (E)
trying to stop you from escaping.

To move around the dungeon use the arrow keys.
To interact with something just move to it.
If you need any help just type 'h'.
